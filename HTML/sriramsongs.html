Sid Sriram is a highly acclaimed Indian playback singer, known for his soulful and emotive voice that has captured the hearts of millions. Born in Chennai and raised in the United States, Sid seamlessly blends Indian classical music with Western influences, creating a unique and contemporary sound. He first gained widespread recognition with the song "Adiye" from the Tamil film *Kadal* (2013), composed by A. R. Rahman, which became an instant hit and established him as a rising star in the Indian music industry. Sid's ability to convey deep emotions through his singing has made him a favorite among fans of Tamil, Telugu, and Malayalam cinema. His songs like "Thalli Pogathey," "Inkem Inkem Inkem Kaavaale," and "Samajavaragamana" have become chartbusters, earning him numerous awards and accolades. Beyond film music, Sid Sriram is also a trained Carnatic vocalist and frequently performs live concerts, showcasing his versatility and deep-rooted passion for music.
